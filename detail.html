Brighten up your garden this year
The pots are made of innovative and durable fiber-stone and fiber-clay material, which give them increased resistance to climatic conditions. They can be left outdoors year-round and are UYV and Frost Resistant. They do not lose their performance or noble appearance for many years, while maintaining their unique authentic look.
Enhance your garden with IDEALIST
The IDEALIST pot is a combination of sophistication, offering a practical and functional modern design all whilst being affordably low priced. These pots’ design and functionality are created specifically for the UK market, and are ideal for decorating gardens, patios, and finally residential and commercial spaces.
Design for Victorian and contemporary gardens and homes

Elegant and stylish IDEALIST pots will fit into any space in a Victorian or modern home. The stylish design of the flowerpots helps draw attention to the natural beauty of the plants themselves, making the plants a statement item.

Thanks to a large selection of various shapes, styles and sizes, it is not difficult to choose pots for decorating your space at home, office or garden. IDEALIST planters quickly and easily transform any space they are put into!

Superior quality without the weight
The IDEALIST pot possesses first and foremost an impressive appearance. Its matte surface, with its characteristic texture, perfectly conveys the texture of the stone. This material not only gives the pots a solid look, but also provides excellent performance. However, despite the large and bulky appearance, the weight of the pots is designed so they can be easily moved from place to place throughout the garden or premises.
Suitable for outdoor use

IDEALIST pots are made of high-quality composite materials, which doesn’t impede air circulation in the soil or the respiration of the roots. It doesn’t interact with the flower primer, therefore doesn’t corrode or lead to souring.

The pots are resistant to UV rays and sudden temperature changes, so they can be used to decorate in open spaces. With IDEALIST pots, your garden will look stylish all year long!

Produced using recycled materials
IDEALIST pots are made from environmentally friendly materials using innovative technologies. Using them in the decoration of your home and garden spaces, you support nature not only in the garden, but throughout the world, because the pots are made from recycled materials and can also be recycled in the future.

Information

Free delivery on all orders shipped to the UK Mainland.

When will I receive my order?

Delivery estimations are in working days, and the estimated delivery time frame for each product is shown below the price tag on the product page.
Small parcels are shipped via DPD next day service, in this case you will receive a text message and notification email with all delivery details and options available to you.
Large, heavy and oversized items are delivered via the pallet network, you will receive a call from our allocated carrier to book in a convenient delivery date. Such deliveries are made from 9 am to 5 pm and kerbside/drive only.

Is express delivery available?

Certain items which we have in our Dunstable warehouse can be delivered next day or even same day if it’s in London or nearby. Please call us to arrange this service.

Can I collect myself?

We offer free collection from our Dunstable warehouse with the following address:
B2/B3 Portland Close, Houghton Regis, LU5 5AW. You will receive an email once your order is ready for collection.

Will I receive a timeslot for my delivery?

For small parcels DPD will provide you an estimated time slot with an option to change if you are not available. You can choose to defer your order to be left in a safe place or collect from your local depot if you are not available for the delivery.
Pallet deliveries are made from 9am to 5pm unless you have upgraded to a timed delivery slot, which can be one via our customer service, please be aware that there is a surcharge to book in a timed slot.

Are instruction provided to me on how to accept pallet deliveries?

Yes, our operations manager will send out instructions via email on how to accept pallet goods. Please be on the look out for this email when your goods have been dispatched from our warehouse.

Can I choose a specific delivery date before my order is despatched?

Yes, please call us to discuss to choose a convenient date, this is a free service provided by Getpotted.com
